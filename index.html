Good to Know

Dress Code

Traditional Indian ethnic wear is warmly encouraged. Think vibrant sarees, lehengas, sherwanis & kurtas. Formal western attire is equally welcome.

🚗

Parking

Ample parking is available at both venues. Royal Wedding Lawn has dedicated parking on NH-7. Please carpool where possible for convenience.

Accommodation

Several hotels are available near Royal Wedding Lawn on Kamptee Road, Nagpur. Please book early. Contact us for recommendations.
